Mission

To build an interdisciplinary collective of individuals, as a platform for engaging in dialogue and collaboration about design.

Our Story

medium(n.)

an agency or means to do something

Medium was founded in 2015 by 3 AAP students who decided to transform a design publication into a live event. Together, they created RAW EXPO, a design exhibition in which students from all across campus showcased their work. The evnet was a huge hit - 500+ attendees proved that there was an incredible amount of interest and need for an interdisciplinary design group. From RAW, Medium Design Collective spawned, to become a community of designers who collaborate on innovative projects and serve as a platform and resource for design dialogue.
